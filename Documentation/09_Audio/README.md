---

# Purpose

Defines the Audio module of Gaia Engine.

Audio transforms simulation events into sound, improving immersion and player feedback without affecting gameplay.

---

# Scope

This module defines:

- sound effects
- ambient audio
- music
- audio events
- spatial audio

It does not define:

- gameplay
- AI
- simulation
- rendering

---

# Philosophy

Audio reflects the world.

The simulation remains authoritative.

Audio never modifies gameplay or simulation.

---

# Responsibilities

The Audio module is responsible for:

- playing sound effects
- managing ambient sounds
- controlling music
- spatializing sounds
- mixing audio

The Audio module never:

- execute gameplay logic
- modify simulation
- alter AI decisions


---

# Design Principles

## Event Driven

Audio reacts to Events generated by Gameplay and Simulation.

---

## Immersion

Sound should reinforce environmental conditions.

Examples:

- Rain
- Wind
- Forest
- Rivers
- Animal Calls

---

## Readability

Important gameplay events should always have recognizable audio cues.

---

## Performance

Audio must remain lightweight on mobile devices.

---
