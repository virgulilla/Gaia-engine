# ORG-007 — Needs

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Approved

**Owner:** Gaia Engine Team

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-29

---

# Purpose

Defines the biological needs that drive future organism behaviour.

Needs are generated by physiology and environmental conditions.

---

# Scope

This specification defines:

- hunger
- hydration
- rest
- reproductive urge

It does not define:

- action selection
- pathfinding
- feeding resolution

---

# Philosophy

Needs are state, not behaviour.

AI systems read needs but never own them.

---

# Fields

- Hunger
- Hydration
- Rest
- Reproduction Urge

All need values use deterministic integer ranges.

Higher values represent higher urgency.

---

# Update Rules

Needs are updated during the Organism Update phase.

Typical influences include:

- metabolism
- water efficiency
- age
- world conditions

---

# Determinism

Identical physiology and world conditions must always generate identical needs.

---

# Serialization

Needs are fully serializable.

---

# Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Needs store deterministic urgency values.
- [ ] Needs contain data only.
- [ ] Needs are serializable.
- [ ] Needs are independent from AI logic.
