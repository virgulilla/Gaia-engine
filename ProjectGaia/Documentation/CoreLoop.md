# PG-002 — Core Loop

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Approved

**Owner:** Gaia Engine Team

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-27

---

# Purpose

Defines the primary gameplay loop of Project Gaia.

The Core Loop explains how the player continuously interacts with the living world generated by Gaia Engine.

---

# Scope

This specification defines:

- player actions
- gameplay loop
- discovery cycle
- progression flow

It does not define:

- simulation implementation
- engine architecture
- UI implementation

---

# Philosophy

The gameplay loop is based on scientific observation.

The player gains knowledge rather than power.

Every action should encourage curiosity.

---

# Core Loop

```text
Observe

↓

Identify

↓

Analyze

↓

Discover

↓

Understand

↓

Unlock New Tools

↓

Observe Again
```

The loop never truly ends.

Greater understanding allows deeper observation.

---

# Player Actions

The player primarily performs six actions:

- Observe
- Inspect
- Compare
- Experiment
- Record
- Learn

The player rarely performs direct control.

---

# Observation

Observation is the most common activity.

Players monitor:

- organisms
- ecosystems
- weather
- populations
- interactions

No rewards are granted simply for waiting.

Observation becomes meaningful when something is discovered.

---

# Inspection

Players may inspect:

- organisms
- species
- genomes
- environments
- statistics

Inspection reveals available knowledge.

Unknown information remains hidden until discovered.

---

# Experimentation

Players may introduce controlled changes.

Examples include:

- modifying climate
- introducing species
- altering resources
- changing terrain

Experiments create new opportunities for discovery.

---

# Discovery

A discovery occurs when the player observes something previously unknown.

Examples:

- a new species
- a new behavior
- a mutation
- an ecosystem collapse
- a symbiotic relationship

Discoveries are permanent.

---

# Knowledge

Knowledge is stored inside the Encyclopedia.

Knowledge unlocks:

- new analysis tools
- advanced statistics
- scientific reports
- deeper biological information

Knowledge is the primary progression system.

---

# Progression Loop

```text
Knowledge

↓

Better Tools

↓

Better Observation

↓

More Discoveries

↓

More Knowledge
```

---

# Failure

There is no traditional failure state.

Ecosystems may collapse.

Species may become extinct.

These outcomes are part of the simulation and become opportunities for learning.

---

# Relationship With Gaia Engine

Gaia Engine generates the simulation.

Project Gaia transforms simulation events into meaningful gameplay.
