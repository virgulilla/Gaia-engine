# PG-001 — Game Vision

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Approved

**Owner:** Gaia Engine Team

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-27

---

# Purpose

Defines the vision of Project Gaia.

This document explains the experience the game aims to deliver rather than its technical implementation.

---

# Vision Statement

Project Gaia is a scientific ecosystem simulation where the player observes, studies and influences the evolution of life without directly controlling individual organisms.

The objective is not to win.

The objective is to understand.

---

# Player Fantasy

The player is not a god.

The player is not a character.

The player is an observer capable of introducing controlled changes into a living world.

Life evolves independently.

The player discovers it.

---

# Core Experience

The game should encourage:

- curiosity
- observation
- experimentation
- learning
- long-term exploration

The player should constantly ask:

> "What happens if...?"

---

# Pillars

## Living World

The ecosystem exists independently of the player.

Species survive, evolve and disappear without scripted events.

---

## Scientific Discovery

Knowledge is progression.

Observing new behaviors is more valuable than collecting resources.

---

## Emergent Gameplay

Interesting situations emerge naturally from simulation.

Scripted content should be minimized.

---

## Meaningful Intervention

The player influences the world without taking complete control.

Every intervention has consequences.

---

## Relaxed Pace

Project Gaia is designed as a thoughtful experience.

Players should feel encouraged to observe rather than react quickly.

---

# Target Audience

Project Gaia is designed for players who enjoy:

- simulation games
- sandbox experiences
- science
- biology
- discovery
- experimentation

---

# Long-Term Goal

The player gradually builds a complete understanding of a living planet through exploration and observation.

Completion is measured by knowledge rather than domination.

---

# Success Criteria

The game succeeds when:

- players become emotionally invested in ecosystems
- discoveries feel meaningful
- each world tells a unique story
- replayability emerges naturally from simulation

---

# Relationship With Gaia Engine

Gaia Engine creates the world.

Project Gaia transforms that world into a player experience.
